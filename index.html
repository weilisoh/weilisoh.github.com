About us

Our Philosophy

We believe that learning is the key to success and to provide learning services that will better students. We aspire to provide students the opportunity to receive the best teaching at the lowest price possible. For parents, we want to provide a hassle-free and safe service, to provide qualified tutors to your doorstep. We wish for parents to feel at peace when using our service. As for tutors, we want to support as many tutors as possible by recommending students, which will be required at a reasonable commission to sustain our service.

Our Commitment

For the students, we are devoted to find qualified teachers to assist your learning. For parents, we are committed in providing a safe and reliable platform for parents to find suitable tutors for your child. For tutors, we are committed in assisting the growth of your tutoring career. We accept constant feedback and provide client support to ensure tutors meet the specific requirements of the student.

Our Tutors

A network of talents including full time, part time tutors, current and retired school teachers, high achievers. Tutors are carrefully selected and screened to ensure they are up to the standards required by students.

Our tutoring sessions are priced according to the type of syllabus, the level of learning and the tutor's experience.
