Apply as Tutor Form

If you are passionate in teaching, and want to help students in Malaysia to learn, join us now! We are looking for passionate, responsible and qualified tutors to join our team. Fill in the form below and we will contact you for any job opportunities available. Kindly read and accept the Terms and Conditions below before you apply as tutor.

Terms and Conditions (for Tutors)

  1. Eligibility
    Tutor must be a Malaysian Citizen. Minimum academic qualification and acceptable grades are required for the subjects that tutor wish to teach. Tutor must also have adequate teaching experiences and be confident, passionate and responsible in teaching.
  2. Commission
    50% commission will be charged on the tutor for the 1st month of tuition fees as a referral fee. Fees received for the subsequent months will be 100% received by tutor.
    If tuition is terminated/cancelled within 1 month, 30% of the total fees collected is charged instead.
  3. Termination
    Tutors whom exhibit irresponsible actions, refuse to co-operate or receive complains from parents/students will be terminated.
  4. Important Notes for Tutors:
    Tutors are required to travel to students' designated areas at own expense.
    Tutors must have own transport.
    Tutors must always be punctual for classes.
  5. Liability
    We are not liable for any possible conflict and disagreement between tutors and parents/students as well as any form of damages caused in connection with the use of our website.
